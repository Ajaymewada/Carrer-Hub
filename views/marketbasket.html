Market Basket Analysis

Discover how customer purchasing patterns reveal powerful product relationships.

Description

Market Basket Analysis (MBA) is a data-driven technique used to uncover hidden patterns in customer purchase behaviour. By analysing transaction-level data, MBA identifies products that are frequently bought together, helping businesses make informed decisions related to product bundling, store layout, inventory planning, pricing, and cross-selling opportunities.

Objective

To discover meaningful product associations and leverage these insights to enhance customer experience, drive revenue growth, improve product placement strategies, and strengthen marketing campaigns through targeted recommendations and intelligent cross-sell/upsell strategies.

Tools

  • Excel / Google Sheets for preliminary analysis
  • Python (Pandas, Mlxtend) for algorithm-based processing
  • Tableau / Power BI for interactive dashboards
  • Data Visualization Tools for pattern interpretation

Data Alignment

  • Data Analyst for transaction-level data preparation
  • Business Analyst for requirement alignment & interpretation
  • Category Manager for product hierarchy validation
  • Data Engineer for preprocessing support (if needed)

Key Concepts

  • Support, Confidence & Lift for analysing association strength
  • Apriori & FP-Growth algorithms for efficient rule mining
  • Identifying high-value itemsets and shopping patterns
  • Translating discovered patterns into business strategies

Project Assets

  • Cleaned & structured dataset for analysis
  • Interactive association dashboard
  • Insights & recommendation document
  • Visual charts (heatmaps, network graphs)

Deliverables

  • Dashboard showcasing product associations
  • Data-driven recommendations for cross-sell strategies
  • Executive insights summarising key transactions
  • Visual reports indicating high-lift item combinations.

Estimated Completion Time

8–10 hours

Dataset

  • Customer purchase transactions
  • Product category & hierarchy mapping
  • Item-level descriptive attributes
  • SKU-level transaction sequences

Impact Metrics

  • Increase in revenue via cross-sell opportunities
  • Improved inventory & product placement decisions
  • Customer basket value uplift
  • Better campaign targeting & personalization

Certification Earned

  • Market Basket Analysis Practitioner
  • Data Insights & Visualization Certificate
  • Retail Analytics & Pattern Mining Certification
