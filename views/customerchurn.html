Predicting Customer Churn Rating

Analyze customer behaviour patterns to predict churn risk and identify retention opportunities.

Description

Predicting Customer Churn Rating is a machine learning project focused on identifying customers who are likely to discontinue a service. By analysing behavioural, demographic, transactional, and usage-based factors, the project assigns a churn probability score to each customer, helping businesses proactively retain valuable users.

Objective

To build a predictive model that estimates customer churn risk, enabling companies to implement targeted retention strategies, reduce revenue loss, improve customer satisfaction, and strengthen long-term loyalty. The objective also includes identifying the key drivers behind churn behaviour.

Tools

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-Learn)
  • Jupyter Notebook / VS Code
  • Tableau / Power BI for visualization
  • SQL for data extraction

Data Alignment

  • Customer demographic data
  • Subscription & usage behaviour
  • Support interactions & complaints
  • Payment history & plan information

Key Concepts

  • Classification modeling (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost)
  • Feature engineering for behavioural patterns
  • Churn scoring & rating system design
  • Confusion matrix, ROC-AUC, Precision/Recall

Project Assets

  • Cleaned & engineered dataset
  • Churn rating prediction model
  • Feature importance insights
  • Interactive churn analytics dashboard

Deliverables

  • Churn probability model with rating categories
  • Customer segmentation by churn risk
  • Retention strategy recommendations
  • Visual reports & KPI dashboards

Estimated Completion Time

10–12 hours

Dataset

  • Customer profile & demographic data
  • Service usage logs
  • Contract & billing details
  • Customer support interaction records

Impact Metrics

  • Lower churn rate & customer retention uplift
  • Revenue protection from high-risk accounts
  • Improved targeting of retention campaigns
  • Better understanding of churn drivers

Certification Earned

  • Customer Churn Prediction Analyst
  • Data Science & Machine Learning Certification
  • Customer Behaviour Analytics Certificate
